Welcome to
Kuldip Memorials Computer Academy

A learning hub


Welcome to Kuldip Memorials Computer Academy (KMCA), where we are dedicated to shaping the future of aspiring individuals through comprehensive computer education. At KMCA, we offer a range of courses in computer learning and certification programs, like Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA), Diploma in Computer Application (DCA), Tally ERP, Auto-CAD and many more courses. Our mission extends beyond traditional academics to equip freshly graduated students with industry-ready skills, including proficiency in computer learning, and the enhancement of essential attributes such as personality development and communication skills. At KMCA, we don't just provide education; we empower individuals to thrive in the dynamic landscape of the digital era. Moreover, our forward-thinking vision extends to pioneering training programs in cutting-edge technologies, such as drone technology, with the aim of nurturing a new generation of educators passionate about shaping the technological landscape of tomorrow. Join KMCA, where education meets innovation, and together, we'll explore the limitless possibilities of the digital realm.

Courses offered

PGDCA and DCA

Our Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA) and Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA) programs are meticulously designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and technology with certification from NIELIT. Through a blend of theoretical instruction and practical hands-on training, our PGDCA and DCA programs prepare students to navigate the complexities of the digital landscape with confidence and proficiency. By offering a balanced approach to learning, our PGDCA and DCA programs empower students to become versatile professionals capable of leveraging technology to drive innovation and success in any organizational setting.

Computer Courses for Kids

Launching soon at Kuldip Memorials Computer Academy (KMCA) are exciting and engaging computer programs tailored specifically for kids. These courses aim to introduce young minds to the world of technology in a fun and educational manner. Covering fundamental concepts, our curriculum includes hands-on activities to build computer literacy, creative exploration through MS Paint, development of essential skills with Typing Master, understanding keyboard functionalities, and fostering an awareness of key computer concepts. These courses provide a foundation for future learning and nurture the natural curiosity of young learners in a playful and educational setting at KMCA.
